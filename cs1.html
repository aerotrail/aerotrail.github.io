Case Study: Hadoop to Databricks Migration

Empowering Scalable, Cloud-Native Analytics at Enterprise Scale

Client Overview

  • Industry: Retail Intelligence
  • Region: India & US
  • Partner: Aerotrail Private Ltd.

Business Challenge

The client’s decade-old Hadoop infrastructure was holding them back — sluggish batch jobs, rising costs, and fractured tools across data and ML. They needed to modernize, unify, and accelerate — without disrupting existing operations.

The Migration Journey

Phase 1: Discovery & Alignment – Through collaborative workshops and platform diagnostics, Aerotrail mapped pain points and priorities. Cross-team input revealed bottlenecks in data refresh cycles and limitations in model experimentation. This became the blueprint for a phased Databricks adoption.

Phase 2: Foundation & Architecture – A scalable Databricks workspace was set up with Unity Catalog for governance. CI/CD pipelines ensured reproducibility, while environment isolation gave teams safe space to build and test.

Phase 3: Pilot Conversion – High-value Spark and Hive jobs were rebuilt using Delta Lake. The ML team began using MLflow for reproducible experiments, significantly reducing time-to-model.

Phase 4: Full Migration – 80+ TB of data was migrated from HDFS to cloud storage with integrity checks, lineage validation, and schema harmonization. Teams worked jointly to resolve data quality flags during cutover.

Phase 5: Enablement & Training – 40+ data professionals were trained in Databricks SQL, Python notebooks, Delta Live Tables, and job orchestration — enabling them to confidently own and evolve the new platform.

Phase 6: Production Cutover – Final workloads were transitioned with zero downtime. Dashboards and alerting ensured smooth handoff. Cost optimization guardrails were introduced for ongoing scalability.

Outcomes & Benefits

Area Before (Hadoop) After (Databricks)
Data Refresh Latency ~4 hours ~25 minutes
Model Experimentation 3–4 days Same-day turnaround
Pipeline Failures 20–25/month <2/month
Query Performance Moderate 3–8x faster
Analyst Productivity Low High

Conclusion

This migration wasn’t just about moving platforms—it was about creating momentum. Aerotrail helped the client embrace a new rhythm of delivery, foster collaboration across teams, and build confidence in their ability to scale, experiment, and adapt quickly in the modern data world.
